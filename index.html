Our Story

Rooted in Dharamshala,
Passionate About Travel

RJ Holidays was founded by local Himachali travel enthusiasts who wanted to share the magic of the mountains with the world. Based on Dharamshala Road, we've been crafting unforgettable journeys across Kashmir, Himachal Pradesh, Nepal, Spiti and Ladakh for over a decade.

We're not a large faceless corporation — we're your personal travel family. Every itinerary is hand-designed, every hotel is personally inspected, and every driver and guide is vetted by us. When you travel with RJ Holidays, you travel with people who genuinely care.
