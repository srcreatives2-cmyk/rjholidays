Why This Package

Package Highlights

Hand-picked experiences that capture Kashmir's soul — curated by guides who've lived here all their lives.

Dal Lake Shikara Ride

Glide across the iconic Dal Lake at sunrise or sunset in a hand-carved shikara and witness the floating gardens.

Gulmarg Snow & Gondola

Asia's highest gondola ride to Apharwat peak. In winter it's a world-class ski destination; in summer a green meadow paradise.

Pahalgam Valley

The "Valley of Shepherds" — Betaab Valley, Aru Valley, and Chandanwari are straight from a Bollywood dream.

Mughal Gardens

Nishat Bagh, Shalimar Bagh & Chashme Shahi — terraced gardens built by Mughal emperors along the Dal lakeshore.

Heritage Houseboat Stay

Two nights aboard a cedarwood houseboat with carved interiors — an experience unique to Kashmir.

Kashmiri Cuisine

Enjoy Wazwan multi-course meals — Rogan Josh, Dum Aloo, Gushtaba, and the iconic Noon Chai.

Day-by-Day Plan

Detailed Itinerary

A carefully paced 7-day journey — never rushed, always memorable.

1
Arrival Day

Arrive Srinagar – Houseboat Check-in

Arrive at Srinagar Airport. Our representative greets you and transfers you to your heritage houseboat on Dal Lake. Evening shikara ride through the Dal Lake floating market. Welcome Kashmiri dinner on the houseboat.

  • Airport pick-up & transfer to Dal Lake
  • Houseboat check-in & welcome tea
  • Evening shikara ride (45 min)
Dinner
2
Srinagar City

Mughal Gardens & Old City

After a sunrise shikara breakfast, visit Nishat Bagh, Shalimar Bagh, and Chashme Shahi gardens. Afternoon in the Old City — Jama Masjid, Shah Hamdan Shrine, and the famous Lal Chowk market for saffron, pashmina, and dry fruits.

  • Nishat Bagh & Shalimar Bagh (terraced Mughal gardens)
  • Shankaracharya Temple (hilltop views)
  • Old City heritage walk & market
BreakfastDinner
3
Gulmarg Day-trip

Gulmarg – Gondola & Snow

Drive 54 km to Gulmarg (2,690 m). Take the Gulmarg Gondola — Phase 1 to Kongdoori, Phase 2 to Apharwat peak (3,979 m) for panoramic Himalayan views. Snow play, sledging, and optional skiing. Return to Srinagar houseboat by evening.

  • Gulmarg Gondola ride (Phase 1 & 2)
  • Biosphere Reserve nature walk
  • Snow activities (seasonal)
BreakfastDinner
4
Pahalgam Transfer

Srinagar → Pahalgam via Saffron Fields

Check out from houseboat and drive 96 km to Pahalgam (2,130 m), passing through Pampore's saffron fields and Avantipura ruins. Hotel check-in. Evening stroll along the Lidder River.

  • Pampore saffron fields (Oct–Nov seasonal)
  • Avantipura ruins (10th-century temple)
  • Pahalgam hotel check-in
BreakfastDinner
5
Valley Explorations

Betaab Valley, Aru & Chandanwari

Full day exploring Pahalgam's scenic valleys. Betaab Valley (named after the Bollywood film) is a meadow of wildflowers ringed by pine. Aru Village is a trekker's haven. Chandanwari is the starting point of the Amarnath Yatra pilgrimage.

  • Betaab Valley picnic
  • Aru Village & Aru Lake short trek
  • Chandanwari snow point visit
BreakfastDinner
6
Return to Srinagar

Pahalgam → Srinagar & Shopping

Morning at leisure in Pahalgam. Drive back to Srinagar. Afternoon reserved for last-minute shopping — Kashmiri carpets, walnut-wood crafts, pashmina shawls, and saffron at the government emporiums and local markets. Farewell Wazwan dinner.

  • Free time at Pahalgam Lidder river
  • Srinagar shopping & handicraft market
  • Farewell Wazwan dinner
BreakfastDinner
7
Departure

Srinagar Airport Drop-off

After breakfast, check out from hotel and transfer to Srinagar Airport for your onward journey. Trip ends with memories to last a lifetime.

When to Go

Best Time to Visit Kashmir

Kashmir is beautiful in every season — each one offers a completely different experience.

🌸

Spring

March – May

Tulip gardens in full bloom, mild weather, ideal for sightseeing. Best overall.

☀️

Summer

June – August

Pleasant 20–25°C, lush green valleys, all roads open. Peak tourist season.

🍂

Autumn

Sep – November

Golden chinar trees, saffron harvest in Pampore. Magical & less crowded. Our favourite.

❄️

Winter

Dec – February

Gulmarg ski season, snowfall in Srinagar. Cold but incredibly romantic.
