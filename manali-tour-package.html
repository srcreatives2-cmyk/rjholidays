Manali Tour Package from Delhi

Manali is one of India’s most beautiful hill stations located in Himachal Pradesh. It is famous for snow-capped mountains, adventure activities, honeymoon trips, and scenic valleys. RJ Holidays offers fully customised Manali tour packages including hotels, cab services, sightseeing, and complete travel support.

Day 1: Delhi to Manali

Overnight journey by Volvo or private cab.

Day 2: Arrival & Local Sightseeing

Day 3: Solang Valley & Adventure Activities

Paragliding, snow activities, ropeway ride.

Day 4: Atal Tunnel / Rohtang Pass

Full day excursion to snow point and scenic valleys.

Day 5: Return to Delhi

Checkout and return journey.
